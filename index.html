About Me
The Designer Behind The Pixels

Hey — I'm Hafiz Moeen Raza, a WordPress & UI/UX Designer based in Rahim Yar Khan, Pakistan. I've spent the last 5+ years turning client visions into high-performing digital products — websites that don't just look great, but actually convert visitors into customers.

My journey started with graphic design fundamentals at SICS Institute, deepened through a PSDF Certified Program in website design, and evolved into UI/UX prototyping through the E-Hunar program. Today, I bring that full-stack design perspective to every project — from wireframe to live deployment.

I specialize in real estate websites, eCommerce stores, and corporate business sites. My process is meticulous: pixel-perfect layouts, fast performance, mobile-first responsiveness, and a deep focus on user experience that keeps visitors engaged.

Beyond design and development, I also bring AI-powered chatbot integration to the table — connecting ChatGPT and Claude APIs into WordPress sites to create intelligent assistants that handle customer queries, capture leads, and keep visitors engaged around the clock.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring UI patterns in games, watching food aesthetics, or on the road exploring new places.
